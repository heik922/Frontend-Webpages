Forkify

JavaScript

An application that allow users search and load recipe from a public API. Users may bookmark their favorite recipes and modify the serving amount. Users may also upload thier own recipe to the API.

Rules and Features :

1.User may search for recipes with certain keywords (e.g. pizza, burger, avocado...)

2.If user want to calculate the ingredients quantity for a different serving number, simpliy just press on the "-" or "+" buttons.

3. User can bookmark the recipe, it will be saved into bookmark list on the top-right corner and easier to search for it.

4. User are also allow to upload their own recipe, with certain rules and ingredients format. Then, the recipe will be upload and stores in API.

5. Only current user may view their own recipes, other user may not search and view the current user's recipes.
